# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 23, 2026
**Last updated:** May 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data PremiumSportsAi collects, how we use it, and the rights you have. It applies to premiumsportsai.com and related services.

## 1. Data we collect

| Category | What | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Account | Email, hashed password (or OAuth ID), handle, favorite teams, tier | Authenticate, personalize, deliver Service |
| Payment | Last 4 of card, billing ZIP, Stripe customer ID — **we never see or store full card numbers** | Process subscriptions and pool entries via Stripe |
| Behavioral | Pages visited, picks viewed, takes posted, contest entries, click events on affiliate links | Improve product, calculate clout, measure conversions |
| Device | IP address, user agent, approximate geo (country/state from IP) | Security, geo-gating for state-by-state sportsbook eligibility |
| Communications | Email open/click events, push notification opt-in tokens | Deliver requested communications |
| AI prompts | Chat messages you send to the AI agent, contest pick history | Generate responses, improve model quality (de-identified) |

We do **not** collect: SSN, government ID, full payment card data, exact GPS location, browsing history outside our site, contact lists, microphone, or camera data.

## 2. How we use it

- Provide and improve the Service
- Process payments and pay out pool winners
- Personalize predictions, recaps, and recommendations
- Send transactional emails (signups, receipts, contest results) and, with consent, marketing emails (daily free pick, upsells)
- Detect fraud, abuse, and ToS violations
- Comply with legal obligations (tax reporting for pool winnings ≥ IRS thresholds)

## 3. Who we share it with

- **Stripe** — payment processing (no card data held by us)
- **Resend** — transactional and marketing email delivery
- **Anthropic** — chat messages and prompt content sent for AI inference (subject to Anthropic's enterprise data policy; not used by them for model training under our agreement)
- **Cloudflare** — hosting, security, DNS, CDN
- **The Odds API, SportsDataIO** — receive only the queries needed to fetch lines/scores; no user data
- **Sportsbook affiliates** — when you click an affiliate link, the sportsbook may receive your IP and a referral ID; what they collect after that is governed by their privacy policy
- **Legal authorities** — when compelled by valid legal process

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** share data with advertisers for targeted advertising.

## 4. Cookies and tracking

We use first-party cookies for session, preference, and A/B testing. We use Cloudflare's privacy-respecting analytics (no cross-site tracking). We do **not** use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or third-party advertising cookies.

You can clear cookies in your browser at any time; doing so will sign you out.

## 5. Push notifications

Push subscriptions are stored as Web Push tokens tied to your user account. You can revoke at any time in browser settings or via the Service. We send: pre-game briefings, injury alerts, take-grade updates, and contest results — never marketing without separate consent.

## 6. Data retention

- Account data: kept while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of account deletion (some records retained longer for tax, fraud, or legal reasons)
- Payment records: 7 years (US tax law)
- Affiliate click logs: 24 months
- AI chat history: 90 days then automatically purged
- Backups: rotated out within 30 days

## 7. Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you can:
- **Access** the data we have about you — email us, we'll send an export
- **Correct** inaccurate data via your Account page
- **Delete** your account and associated data via Account → Delete Account
- **Export** your data in JSON
- **Opt out** of marketing emails (one-click unsubscribe in every email)
- **Revoke** push notifications

**California residents (CCPA/CPRA):** you have the right to know, delete, correct, opt out of "sharing" (we do not sell or share for advertising), and non-discrimination. Submit requests via email.

**EU/UK residents (GDPR):** legal bases for processing are (a) contract for account and payment data, (b) legitimate interest for security and product improvement, (c) consent for marketing emails and push notifications. You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Our representative is reachable at the email below.

**Account deletion**: `Account → Delete Account` triggers immediate purge of profile, takes, picks, chat history, and push tokens. Payment ledger entries are retained per Section 6.

## 8. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 21. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn we have, we will delete it.

## 9. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (Cloudflare D1, R2). Passwords are hashed with bcrypt or Argon2. Stripe handles all card data. We perform regular security reviews and use the principle of least privilege for internal access.

No service is 100% secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we will notify you within 72 hours per applicable law.

## 10. International transfers

Our infrastructure runs on Cloudflare's global edge. Data may be processed in the US and other countries. For EU/UK users, transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses with our subprocessors.

## 11. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be announced via email and a banner on the site at least 14 days before taking effect.
