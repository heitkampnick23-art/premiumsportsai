# Responsible Gambling

We want PremiumSportsAi to be fun, informative, and **never harmful**. Gambling is entertainment, not income. Bet only with money you can afford to lose.

## Warning signs

If any of these describe you or someone you know, please get help today:

- Betting more than planned, or chasing losses
- Borrowing money to bet, or using money meant for bills
- Lying to family or friends about how much or how often you bet
- Feeling anxious, depressed, or irritable when not betting
- Trying to quit and failing
- Betting interfering with work, sleep, school, or relationships

## Get help now (US)

| Resource | Contact |
|---|---|
| **National Problem Gambling Helpline** | Call/text **1-800-GAMBLER** (1-800-426-2537), 24/7, free, confidential |
| **NCPGambling.org** | https://www.ncpgambling.org/ — resources, treatment locator, chat |
| **Gamblers Anonymous** | https://www.gamblersanonymous.org/ — peer support meetings |
| **GamTalk** | https://gamtalk.org/ — anonymous online community |

Outside the US: visit https://www.begambleaware.org/ (UK) or your country's national problem-gambling helpline.

## Self-exclusion at the sportsbook level

We strongly encourage anyone struggling to **self-exclude at the sportsbook**. State self-exclusion lists prevent licensed sportsbooks (DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, ESPN BET, etc.) from accepting your wagers, and ban you from receiving promotional offers. Most US states with legal sports betting offer this — search "[your state] gambling self-exclusion."

## Set your own limits

Before you bet, set:
- **A weekly bankroll** you can afford to lose
- **A maximum bet size** as a % of bankroll (Kelly suggests fractional — most pros use 1-3%)
- **A stop-loss trigger** (e.g., quit for the week after losing X%)
- **A time limit** per day on betting-related browsing

The Bet Lab on this site reports Kelly stake sizing for every model edge. Treat it as a **maximum**, not a target.
